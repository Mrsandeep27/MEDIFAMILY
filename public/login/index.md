# MediFamily — Your Whole Family's Health, In Your Pocket

## What is MediFamily?

MediFamily is a free family health app that works without internet. One account to manage prescriptions, medicines, lab reports, and emergency info for your entire family — parents, spouse, kids, grandparents.

## Why MediFamily?

### The Problem
- Doctor asked for last year's prescription. Can't find it.
- Papa's medicine ran out. Forgot which one.
- Emergency at hospital. Nobody remembers blood group or allergies.
- Visited 3 doctors. Had to repeat medical history each time.

### The Solution
One app. Everyone's health. Always on your phone.

## Key Features

### 1. Family-First
One login for your whole family. Papa's BP medicines, Maa's sugar reports, your lab tests, kids' vaccinations — all in one place.

### 2. Works Offline
No internet? No problem. Your records are stored on your phone and sync when you're online. Perfect for tier-2/tier-3 cities.

### 3. Emergency Card
Blood group, allergies, medicines, emergency contact — one tap, shareable via WhatsApp. Show QR code to doctors in emergencies.

### 4. Prescription Scanner
Scan paper prescriptions using your phone camera. Supports Hindi and English handwriting. AI extracts medicine names, dosages, and schedules automatically.

### 5. Medicine Reminders
Never miss a dose. Daily/weekly reminders with adherence tracking, streaks, and a visual calendar.

### 6. AI Health Assistant
Ask health questions in Hindi or English. Get symptom assessment, possible causes, home remedies, and when to see a doctor.

### 7. Vitals Tracking
Track BP, blood sugar, weight, temperature, and SpO2 for every family member. Charts with normal range indicators.

### 8. Doctor Sharing
Share health records with doctors via secure QR code link. No app needed on doctor's side. Links expire automatically.

## How It Works

1. **Sign up** — Free, takes 30 seconds (email or Google)
2. **Add your family** — Name, relation, blood group, allergies, conditions
3. **Store everything** — Prescriptions, lab reports, medicines, bills
4. **Access anywhere** — Offline on your phone, sync across devices when online

## Pricing

Free forever. No ads. No subscription. No data selling. Your health data stays on your phone.

## Tech

- Progressive Web App (PWA) — works in Chrome, Safari, any browser
- No Play Store download needed — open medifamily.in and install
- Data encrypted and stored locally on your device
- Cloud sync via Supabase (optional, when online)
- Built with Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
